Our Story

Mobile World has been serving the Visakhapatnam community for years, providing expert mobile phone repair services with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Founded by Sanjay Agarwal and Sunil Agarwal, we started with a simple mission: to provide fast, reliable, and affordable mobile repair services that our customers can trust.

What sets us apart is our innovative free pickup and delivery service - we understand your time is valuable, so we come to you! Our expert technicians handle all repairs with care and precision, using only quality parts and proven techniques.
