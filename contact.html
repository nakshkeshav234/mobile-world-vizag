Frequently Asked Questions

Is the pickup and delivery service really free?

Yes! We provide completely free pickup and delivery service within Visakhapatnam city limits.

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. Some repairs like screen replacement can be done the same day depending on parts availability.

Do you provide warranty on repairs?

Yes! All our repairs come with 3-6 months warranty depending on the type of repair and parts used.

What brands do you repair?

We repair all major brands including Apple, Samsung, OnePlus, Xiaomi, Vivo, Oppo, Realme, Motorola, and more.

Do I need to pay any advance?

No advance payment required. You pay only after the repair is completed and you're satisfied with the service.

What if my phone can't be repaired?

We'll diagnose your phone for free and inform you if it's repairable. If it can't be repaired, there's no charge for diagnosis.
